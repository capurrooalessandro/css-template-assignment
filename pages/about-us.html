Great ideas happen anywhere. We help you bring them together.

SyncFlow was founded on a simple belief: the best teams shouldn't be limited by geography. We build intuitive, beautiful tools that make remote collaboration feel as natural as being in the same room.

Our Story

It all started with a frustrating project. Our co-founders, Nyeleti, a UX designer from South Africa, and Alessandro, a developer from Italy, were trying to collaborate on a complex app build. Juggling endless video calls, messy email threads, and tools that just weren't built for creative feedback, they knew there had to be a better way. They decided to build a simple internal tool to solve their own problem: a shared visual space that was fast, intuitive, and put the work front and centre.

That simple tool quickly became indispensable to their workflow. They soon realized they weren't the only ones facing these challenges. After sharing a beta version with other remote creatives and receiving overwhelmingly positive feedback, they knew they had something special. In 2022, they officially founded SyncFlow, a remote-first company dedicated to helping creative teams around the world do their best work, together.
